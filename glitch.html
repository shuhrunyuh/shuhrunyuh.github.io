GLITCH ART






"It's Not A Barbie World"

My glitch artwork is aimed to be a commentary on the unrealistic beauty standards Kylie Jenner displays on social media. Her massive fan base only sees this perfectly curated image with little to no imperfections, or the Barbie version of herself. It is mostly unattainable since it requires a ridiculous amount of wealth and stealth. It's one thing to embrace the changes you have had done, but it is another to hide it and pretend like you naturally look that way. I chose to glitch her Barbie Halloween photoshoot to show that her image isn't what it really seems to be. It's been built to be that way.
